# QR Codes GST APIs

You can use Razorpay QR Codes to create QR Codes and share them with customers to accept digital payments.

## How it Works 

Given below is the flow:
1. You create a QR Code.
1. A short URL is generated. 
1. Share the URL with your customers.
1. Customers scan the QR Code with their preferred UPI PSP app and complete the payment.
