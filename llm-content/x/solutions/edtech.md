# EdTech

Manage payments and financial operations of your educational institution from a consolidated dashboard. Create an online platform, track payments, and create payouts within minutes.

### Education and E-Learning

Educational institutions and e-learning platforms can leverage RazorpayX for hassle-free fee collection. Automated receipts, timely reminders, and an easy-to-use interface simplify the payment process for students and parents.

  
### Payout Links for Guest Instructors

     - Simplify payments using [Payout Links](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/payout-links.md), streamlining finance procedures.
     - Avoid bank details for one-time resources while maintaining proper records.
    

  
### Easy Accounting Integration

     - Easy reconciliation of multiple payouts.
     - Utilize [Accounting Integration](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/accounting.md) with [Zoho Books](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/accounting/zohobooks/account-statement.md) and [Tally](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/accounting/tally/account-statement.md), along with downloadable [Account Statements](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/account-statement.md).
     - Enhance visibility, transparency, and team collaboration while ensuring consented access.
    

  
### Manage Team and Custom User Roles

     - Assign roles and permissions to both vendors and freelancers, enhancing security and control.
     - [Custom User Roles](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/manage-teams/create-user-role.md) allow controlled access with or without [2FA](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/manage-teams/2fa.md).
     
    

  
### Bulk Payouts for Refunds and Scholarships

     - Use [RazorpayX Lite or Current Account](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/account-types.md) and [Bulk Payout Links](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/payout-links/bulk.md) for convenient student incentive payouts. 
     - Simplify rewards distribution using [Payout Links](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/x/payout-links.md) without the need for individual account details, improving efficiency.
