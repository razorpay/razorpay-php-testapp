# Payroll Changelog

- **API Changelog**: Discover new releases and updates regarding Razorpay APIs.

  - **RazorpayX Changelog**: Discover new releases and updates regarding RazorpayX products.

The following table records changes made to RazorpayX Payroll since Jan 2024:

Month-Year | Feature | Description
---
April 2025 | Form 24Q | Payroll introduced check for  [ accurate 24Q filing.](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/tds/form-24Q.md)
---
March 2025 | Salary Component Library | Create [custom salary components](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/component-library.md) for your organisation.
---
Feb 2025 | Provident Fund | Payroll has resumed automated registration and PF payments for employees. This was paused due to 2FA rule on the PF portal.
---
Dec 2024 | Tax Deductions | Create [investment declaration and proof upload windows](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/tax-deductions-setup.md) for your organisation.
---
Nov 2024 | 2FA | Introduced [2-factor authentication](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/administrator/#two-factor-authentication.md) for the following actions: 
 - [Contractor Payments](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/payroll-payouts/#contractor-payments.md)
- [User role creation](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/user-roles-workflows/#create-user-roles.md)
- [Payroll execution](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/run-payroll/#execute-payroll.md)
- [Pay advance salary](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/advance-salary/#pay-advance-salary.md)
- [Pay reimbursements](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/reimbursements/#pay-reimbursements.md)
- [Create an employee loan](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/loans/#create-a-loan.md)
- API access
  

---
Sep 2024 | Professional Tax | Due to mandatory 2FA implementation by the Karnataka Government, [automated PT payments](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/professional-tax/#pt-payment-for-karnataka-employees.md) via Payroll for employees residing in Karnataka are temporarily paused. 
---
Aug 2024 | Provident Fund (PF) | Due to mandatory 2FA implemented by the EPFO, [automated PF payments and registration](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/faqs/#pf-payments.md) for employees via the Payroll Dashboard is temporarily paused.
---
Aug 2024 | 2FA | Introduced mandatory [2-factor authentication](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/administrator/#two-factor-authentication.md) for administrators and user roles during login when accessing the Payroll Dashboard for secure money movement.
---
Aug 2024 | Budget Changes | Revised the payroll calculations per the [changes introduced](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/run-payroll.md) in the Union Budget for the new regime.
---
July 2024 | LWF | Introduced [enabling LWF for employees](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/statutory-compliance/#labour-welfare-fund-lwf.md) from their profiles.
---
May 2024 | Fund Loading | Introduced Source Account Validation to ensure secure fund transfer and payments.
---
May 2024 | Approval Workflow | You can set up [approval workflow](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payroll/approval-workflow.md) and add approvers for core payroll and salary revision actions.
