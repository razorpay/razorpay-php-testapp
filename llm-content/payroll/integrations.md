# About RazorpayX Payroll Integrations

Payroll partners with leading organisations in the HR/Technology space that specialise in HRMS, attendance, flexible benefits, insurance, and so on to offer ready-to-use integrations. These integrations ensure auto-data synchronisation across systems.
