# Glossary

The following table lists all the commonly used terms and their definitions used in Payment Button:

Terms | Description
---
Fixed Amount | A price field where you decide the amount a customer should pay.
---
Item with Quantity | A price field with a quantity selection widget which facilitates the purchase of multiple quantities.
---
80G | The Section **80G** of the IT Act allows donations made to specified relief funds and charitable institutions as a deduction from gross total income before arriving at taxable income.
---
Stock | The goods or merchandise stored with the business in a warehouse or a storage facility.
---
Expiry Date | The Expiry Date is the date after which the customer cannot pay using the Payment Button.
