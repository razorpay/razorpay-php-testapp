# Close Customer Identifier

Customer Identifiers can be closed in two ways:
- Automatically, by specifying a **Close By** date and time during [Customer Identifier creation](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payments/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/create.md).
- Manually, using the **Close Account** button on the Customer Identifier details pane:
    1. In the list, select the Customer Identifier ID.
    2. In the right pane that appears, click **Close Account**.
    

    ![](/docs/assets/images/smart-collect-va_close.jpg)
    

    3. A dialog box appears to confirm the closing. Click **Yes**.
