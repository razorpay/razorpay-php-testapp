# QR Code States

A QR Code can be in one of two states: `active` or `closed`.

## QR Codes States and Descriptions

The table below lists the various states of a QR Code and gives a brief description of each state:

Status | Description
---
`active` | Indicates that the QR Code has been created and is ready to accept payments. Know more about [creating a QR Code.](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payments/qr-codes/create.md)
---
`closed` | Indicates that the QR Code has been closed. Customers cannot make payments using a closed QR Code. Know more about [closing a QR Code.](@/Applications/MAMP/htdocs/new-docs/llm-content/payments/qr-codes/close.md)
