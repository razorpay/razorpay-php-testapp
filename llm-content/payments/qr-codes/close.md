# Close a QR Code

You can close a QR Code. A customer cannot make payments for the closed QR Codes. Know more about [QR Code States](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/razorpay/razorpay-php-testapp/markdown-docs/llm-content/payments/qr-codes/states.md).

> **INFO**
>
> 
> **Handy Tips**   
> 
> QR Codes that support multiple payments cannot be closed.
> 

## Close a QR Code from the Dashboard

Here is short video on how to close a QR code from the Dashboard.

[Video: https://www.youtube.com/embed/gL9rYd4gv0w?si=NZIuLT42iCeY4_Dk]

To close a QR Code:

1. Log in to the Dashboard.
1. Click on **QR Code**.
1. [Search for the QR Code](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/razorpay/razorpay-php-testapp/markdown-docs/llm-content/payments/qr-codes/search.md) that you want to close using the search criteria.
1. Select the **QR Code ID**.
1. On the right-hand side panel, click the **Close** button.
    
1. On the **Close QR Code?** dialog box, click **Yes** to confirm the closure.
    ![Confirm the closure of a QR code from the Razorpay Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/razorpay/razorpay-php-testapp/markdown-docs/llm-content/assets/images/qr-codes-close-confirm-close.jpg.md)

The closed QR Codes display a **Closed** status label.

![QR code closed from the Razorpay Dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/razorpay/razorpay-php-testapp/markdown-docs/llm-content/assets/images/qr-codes-close-closed.jpg.md)
