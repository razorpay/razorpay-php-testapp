# About Logistics Partners Integration

- **Logistics Partners Integration Changelog**: Discover new features, updates and deprecations related to the Logistics Partners Integration (since Jan 2024).

Your logistics partner is responsible for delivering orders to your customers. You can integrate with Logistics Partners like **Shiprocket**, **Delhivery**, **iThink Logistics**, **Unicommerce** and **ClickPost** with Magic Checkout to easily fetch order status and provide RTO protection on COD orders.

#### Features

- Get the delivery status update periodically.
- Eliminate the need to send these updates or bulk upload them via Dashboard manually.

#### Advantages

- Improve the COD Intelligence you receive in filtering out high RTO risk COD orders.
- Orders processed will be eligible for RTO Insurance (if opted).
