# Different Payment Providers

There are two types of payment providers:
- Bank Gateways: Banks like Axis, ICICI, HDFC, and so on provide their own payment gateway accounts. 
- Aggregators: They work with banks and provide a wide range of payment methods. 

You can add payment providers by adding a few details on the Razorpay Dashboard. Every provider has a different set of prerequisites and details. Before adding a provider, you should ensure that the payment gateway or aggregator is ready and has completed all the prerequisites.
