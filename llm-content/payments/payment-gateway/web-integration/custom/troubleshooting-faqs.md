# Troubleshooting & FAQs

### 1. I am getting errors like “Your Payment was not successful as you have entered invalid card details. To pay successfully try adding the right details” or “Your Payment could not be completed as this bank is not enabled by the business. To complete the payment, use another account.” What should I do?

         You may be experiencing the above errors because your account may not have the necessary payment methods and banks enabled. To enhance your customer experience, please contact our [Support team](https://razorpay.com/support/) and enable the correct methods and banks for your account.
        

    
### 2. Which payment methods appear on Instagram/Facebook browsers?

         UPI Intent is the only payment method that appears on Instagram/Facebook browsers. These browsers do not support payment methods that open in a pop-up page.
