# About Logistics Partners Integration

Your logistics partner is responsible for delivering orders to your customers. You can integrate with Logistics Partners like **Shiprocket**, **Delhivery**, **iThink Logistics**, **Unicommerce** and **ClickPost** with COD & Magic Checkout to easily fetch order status.

#### Features

- Get the delivery status update periodically.
- Eliminate the need to send these updates or bulk upload them via Dashboard manually.

#### Advantages

- Improve the RTO Intelligence you receive in filtering out high RTO risk COD orders.
- Orders processed will be eligible for RTO Insurance (if opted).
