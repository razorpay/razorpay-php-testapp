## Charge per Transfer

A fee of 0.25% is charged per transfer to a linked account.

For example, on a transfer of ₹100 made to a linked account, a
0.25% charge (₹0.25) is applicable. Hence, ₹100.25 will be debited from
the merchant, and the linked account will be credited with an amount of ₹100.
