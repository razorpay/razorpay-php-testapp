### Endpoints

- **post** `/v1/bills` - Create a Bill: 
 Creates a Bill.

- **patch** `/v1/bills/:bill_id` - Update a Bill: 
 Updates the details of a Bill.

- **delete** `/v1/bills/:bill_id` - Delete a Bill: 
 Deletes a Bill.
