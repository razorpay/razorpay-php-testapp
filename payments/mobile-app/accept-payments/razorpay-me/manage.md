# Manage Razorpay.me

You can edit the Razorpay.me URL immediately after account activation only. We recommend that you use the business/brand name that your customers identify with you.

## How to Edit Razorpay.me URL

To edit the URL:
1. Tap **Edit** on the Razorpay.me URL creation screen.
    
2. Modify the URL. You may:
    - Select one from the list of recommended suffixes, or
    - Enter a custom suffix. Ensure the suffix is similar to your business or brand name.
    
> **INFO**
>
> 
>     **Handy Tips**
> 
>         - Characters supported are `a-z` and `0-9`.
>         - The business/brand name cannot exceed 15 characters.
>     

    
3. Tap **Save**.

The Razorpay.me URL has been successfully modified.
