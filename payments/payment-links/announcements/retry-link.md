# Increase Order Conversion Rate with Retry Links

With so much investment in getting customers to visit your website/app, drop-off should be the last problem to have because a payment has failed.

Presenting **Retry Links**, a new feature to help convert customers who dropped off because of failed payments!

## How does it Work?

- When a payment fails on your website, your customers will receive the following notification on the payment gateway. 
    
- They will also receive an SMS with a link to retry the payment.
     
- The link will take them to the checkout page where they can complete their order.
    

This feature will be made available for your account on **9th February 2022**. 
More details and reports will be available under the report section of the Dashboard.

For any questions, feedback, or to turn off this feature for your account, please feel free to contact us at [retrylink@razorpay.com](mailto:retrylink@razorpay.com).
