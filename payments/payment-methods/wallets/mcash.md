# MCash

MCash is a digital wallet and online payment platform designed for Malaysian users. Developed with convenience and security in mind, MCash allows users to effortlessly manage their payments, whether it is for daily transactions, shopping online, or paying bills. It also supports peer-to-peer transfers, making it easier to send and receive money within Malaysia.

To use MCash:

1. Users must first download the MCash app and create an account.
2. They can then link their bank account or credit/debit card to their MCash account and start using the service instantly.

## MCash Payment Flow

Given below is the payment flow for MCash on Razorpay Curlec checkout:

1. The customer selects **Wallets** → **MCash** at checkout.
2. They are redirected to the MCash login page and sign in with their credentials.
3. They complete the authorisation process and select a payment option.
4. Upon payment completion, the customer receives a UI notification and is redirected to your website or app.
