## Choosing the Right Device for Your Industry

When selecting a payment terminal for your specific industry, consider these factors:

1. **Transaction Volume**: Higher transaction volumes benefit from faster processors and larger batteries.
2. **Connectivity Needs**: Consider whether 4G, WiFi, or both are necessary for your business environment.
3. **Form Factor**: Full-size terminals offer more features but mini terminals provide greater portability.
4. **Future-Proofing**: Newer Android versions and higher specifications provide longer useful life.
5. **Industry-Specific Features**: Consider special requirements such as scanning, printing, or durability.
